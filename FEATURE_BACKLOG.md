# Vision

Performance Passport aims to answer a question that existing running platforms do not:

"How good is your running really?"

Instead of simply reporting activities, Performance Passport measures performance after considering context such as weather, terrain, fatigue, recovery and training intent.

The goal is to provide insights that help athletes understand their long-term development rather than simply recording workouts.
